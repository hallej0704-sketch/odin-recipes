Idly

Description

Idli (or idly) is a popular, nutritious South Indian savory cake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter, typically served steamed with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • Ground black lentils
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Ferment the batter
  2. Pour into the idly steamer
  3. Steam till cooked
  4. Let it cool and serve
